Frequently Asked Questions

What is BizQuote and how does it work?
BizQuote is a fast, free quoting platform for anyone looking for services across industries. Users fill a simple form and instantly receive preliminary quotes. Those requests are sent directly to businesses in the selected region, who then follow up with personalized offers.
Is it really free for people who need services?
Absolutely. BizQuote is completely free for service seekers. You don’t need to sign up, and your quote is delivered to you in just a few steps — no hidden fees, no waiting around.
How do businesses get leads from the platform?
Businesses subscribe to a plan based on how many regions they want to cover. When a user in their selected region requests a quote for a service they offer, the business is notified immediately and can respond with a tailored quote.
Can a business cancel or upgrade their plan at any time?
Yes. BizQuote plans are flexible. Businesses can upgrade to expand their reach or cancel if they need to pause. There are no long-term commitments — just opportunities.
